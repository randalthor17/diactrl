Features:


By providing ways to remind yourself to eat, exercise, take your medicine regularly and monitor your progress, we can help you stay healthy and avoid risks.

Calendar

Your very own calendar with:

  1. Daily records and notes - comprising of:
    • Glucose levels
    • Food intake
    • Insulin and medicine intake
    • Exercise details
  2. Weekly Review Notes
    • Weight
    • BMI
  3. Monthly Progress Report

Reminders

  1. Daily reminders:
    • Insulin intake
    • Medicine intake
    • Exercise
  2. Weekly reminders:
    • Glucose level check
    • BP check
  3. Monthly reminders:
    • Progress check
